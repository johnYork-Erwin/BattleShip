Battle Ship!

Rules

Battleship is a game where each player has five ships hidden on a 10x10 grid. The goal of the game is to sink your opponents fleet before they sink yours. The five ships are of different lengths and you must hit each segment of a ship in order to sink it. This site implements the less popular 'SALVO' variation where each surviving ship allows the player an independant shot.

There are five kinds of ships. Their titles and lengths are listed below.

  • Carrier:5
  • Battleship:4
  • Cruiser:3
  • Submarine:3
  • Destroyer:2

Trivia Bonus!

This site also implemented a trivia minigame! At any point in time during the battle you can use one of your three trivia bonus attacks to call in the aid of skilled bomber pilots. However, they will only get the correct coordinates if you answer the question correctly. On a correct answer they will score 3 random hits on the enemy fleet. On an unsuccessful answer they will score 3 misses on the enemy fleet. Either way they've helped you though!
